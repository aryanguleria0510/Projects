Nutrition Tips

Healthy Eating

Focus on whole foods, lean proteins, and plenty of vegetables. Aim for a balanced diet with proper portion control.

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables
  • Choose lean protein sources
  • Limit processed foods

Hydration

Stay hydrated throughout the day. Water is essential for muscle function and recovery.

  • Drink 8-10 glasses daily
  • Increase intake during workouts
  • Monitor urine color

Meal Timing

Time your meals around your workouts for optimal performance and recovery.

  • Eat 2-3 hours before exercise
  • Refuel within 30 minutes after
  • Don't skip breakfast

Calorie Tracking

Track your daily calorie intake to ensure you're meeting your fitness goals.

  • Use a food diary app
  • Read nutrition labels
  • Be consistent with tracking
